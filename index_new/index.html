weLost™, builds a social weight loss network; where friends help friends lose weight.


Weight loss is a social process. It is natural to bring the support of social networks to people losing weight. Our iPhone technology brings the social network to the place it is most needed -- the user's hand.

weLost is simple. We care about one number -- your weight. No calorie counting, no point system, no exercise log is required. We just make it easy for you to track an average daily weight. We're about pushing the red line below your goal. And sharing that success with your friends.

weLost's social weight loss network helps you build weight loss teams and cheerleading squads. It is an electronic hub for weight loss information. We have weight loss doctors blogging about current medical innovations to help you lose weight.

Features

  • Allows entry and tracking of your weight.
  • Calculates the weighted average, so that small fluctuations from day to day don't count against you.
  • Allows you to send messages to facebook and twitter as well as the weLost social network.
  • Gives you easy access to the helpful weightloss blog, Losin' it.
  • It's easy to use!

The red line focuses you on your average weight change, not daily ups and downs.
